Our mission is to protect and conserve the forests and endangered species endemic to Negros and the Philippines.

We are a non-profit organization that relies on donations and sponsors to continue our work. We also have a breeding center for some of the most threatened species of animals where the majority of them are found only in Negros and Panay and nowhere else in the world.
